Field: Repeating Date
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.0.6
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 15 May 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.	Upload the 'repeatingdatefield' folder in this archive to your Symphony
	'extensions' folder.
	
2.	Enable it by selecting the "Field: Repeating Date", choose Enable from the
	with-selected menu, then click Apply.

3.	You can now add the "Repeating Date" field to your sections.
